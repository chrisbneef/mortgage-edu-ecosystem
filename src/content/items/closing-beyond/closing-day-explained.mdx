Closing is the finish line — signing day, when ownership transfers to you.

## The Closing Disclosure

You'll receive your <GlossaryTerm slug="closing-disclosure">Closing Disclosure</GlossaryTerm> at
least three business days before closing. Review it against your Loan Estimate.

<Callout>
Funds for taxes and insurance are often collected into an
<GlossaryTerm slug="escrow">escrow</GlossaryTerm> account and bundled into your monthly payment.
</Callout>

## What to bring

A government ID, your cashier's check or wire confirmation for closing costs, and patience for a
stack of signatures.
