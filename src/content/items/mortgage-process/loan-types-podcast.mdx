In this episode we walk through the major loan programs and who each one fits best.

## What we cover

- **Conventional.** The default for buyers with solid credit and savings.
- **FHA.** Flexible credit, low down payment.
- **VA.** No down payment for eligible veterans and service members.
- **USDA.** Zero-down for eligible rural and suburban areas.
- **Jumbo.** For loan amounts above conforming limits.
