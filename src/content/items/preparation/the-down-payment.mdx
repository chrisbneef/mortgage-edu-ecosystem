The down payment is often the biggest hurdle, but the "20% rule" is more myth than requirement.

## How much you actually need

- **Conventional:** as little as 3% down for qualified buyers.
- **FHA:** 3.5% with flexible credit.
- **VA & USDA:** 0% down for eligible buyers.

<Callout type="warning">
With less than 20% down on a conventional loan you'll usually pay
<GlossaryTerm slug="pmi">PMI</GlossaryTerm> until you build enough equity.
</Callout>

## Gift funds

Many programs let family gift some or all of your down payment, with a documented gift letter.
