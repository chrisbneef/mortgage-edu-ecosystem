# Niche Content Map (10 Embeddable Audience Widgets)

Each niche is a standalone, self-rooting widget at `/#/niche/<slug>` (no link back to the master hub). This map is the search-intent research and the article plan for each. Articles are tagged `niches: [<slug>]` in frontmatter, plus a `journeyCategory` and `phases`.

> Spot-validated against live search (June 2026): DSCR (qualify on rent, 20–25% down, DSCR ≥ 1.0), bank-statement loans (12–24 months of deposits, 20–30% down, ~620 score), HECM (62+, FHA-insured, mandatory counseling, you still owe taxes/insurance), foreign-national (20%+ down, visa, foreign income/assets accepted). Validate long-tail query strings before scaling. Sources at the bottom.

Slugs: `first-time-buyer`, `real-estate-investor`, `veterans-va`, `self-employed`, `jumbo-luxury`, `credit-challenged`, `government-fha-usda`, `renovation-construction`, `reverse-mortgage`, `foreign-national`.
